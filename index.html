Let´s visit whales!

A brief introduction

Hello! I´m from Colombia and I want to tell about a great plan to do if you visit this country. I'm talking about see the amazing, and pretty beautiful humpback whales in the pacific ocean.

If you want to know what can you do there, let me give you some ideas:

To do list

  • Swim in the beach
  • Eat delicious sea food
  • Visit the local fair
  • Do trekking and see waterfalls
  • Of course, admire humpback whales

When I went there, I traveled during a weekend with a vey nice agency trip, call Xperiencia. On the itinerary, they included many local activities, two whale sightings and a lot of boat rides. It was amazing!
